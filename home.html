Puranmal Lahoti Government Institute of Engineering and Technology, Latur is a well-known government technical institute located in Maharashtra and is considered one of the important educational centers in the Marathwada region. It is a government-run institution, which makes it highly affordable compared to private engineering colleges, and it has been serving students for many decades by providing quality technical education in engineering and applied sciences. The institute offers diploma and degree-level engineering programs in various branches such as Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electronics Engineering, and Information Technology. Being affiliated with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere, it follows a structured and industry-relevant curriculum designed to build strong theoretical knowledge along with practical skills. The college is known for its laboratory facilities, workshops, and experienced faculty members who guide students in both academic learning and technical development. Over the years, many students from this institute have gone on to build careers in government jobs, private companies, and higher studies. Because of its government status, disciplined academic environment, and focus on technical education, it remains a preferred choice for students in Maharashtra who want affordable engineering education with decent career opportunities.
